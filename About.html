ABOUT

Two Foot T-Rex is an electrifying band that has taken the music scene by storm. At the heart of their explosive sound is the incredibly talented drummer, Joe Gibson. With rhythm coursing through his veins, Joe's drumming prowess is nothing short of remarkable. He effortlessly commands the beat, infusing each song with a pulsating energy that leaves audiences awe-struck. His impeccable timing, innovative fills, and undeniable passion for his craft make him the rhythmic backbone of Two Foot T-Rex's unforgettable performances. Joe Gibson's drumming is the driving force behind the band's meteoric rise, earning him a well-deserved reputation as one of the most talented drummers in the industry.

Joe Gibson is a drummer extraordinaire, renowned for his exceptional drumming abilities that push the boundaries of traditional percussion. At his last concert, he left the audience in awe when he daringly played his drum kit upside down. Gibson's skillful mastery of rhythm and technique was on full display as he effortlessly navigated the unconventional setup, creating a mesmerizing auditory and visual spectacle that will be remembered for years to come. His innovative approach to drumming continues to inspire and challenge the boundaries of what's possible in the world of music.
